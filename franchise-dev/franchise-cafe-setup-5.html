    Hygiene System

    Food Safety is essential in a food establishments operational procedures. Operational procedures include proper hand washing, systematic usage of chopping boards in food preparation, usage of anti-bacterial chemicals, meticulous temperature control and proper storage. F&B at Work helps clients in the drafting and preparation of SOP for maintaining kitchen and restaurant cleanliness and hygiene standards. The team at F&B at Work is ever ready to share their knowledge in these areas to ensure the hygiene system of clients establishment is tip-top.
