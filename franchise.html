Procedure

QSR Group, A member of ANZ Global Plus JSC Group
Download Application Forms

Step 1 – Inquiry

Do you want to be part of the QSR Group family?
We provide you with basic information about the management of QSR Group, and advise you on the steps to take to join the QSR Group franchise network.

Step 2 – Application

You can apply for a franchise by filling in the application form.

Step 3 – Location Search

Site-finding is a barometer of a successful business!
You can consult over finding the right site with the specialists from QSR Group, who will investigate the floating population/commercial conditions/approaches/general population/appropriateness of the site, etc..

Step 4 – Interview

Field stores are the face of the QSR Group brand!
Owners of QSR Group stores should have enthusiasm for the business as well as a high level of ‘service mindedness’.

Step 5 – Approval

When approved, the applicant shall prepare for an opening with the support of H/Q.

Step 6 – Lease Contract

Owners will carry forward the lease contract of the store.

Step 7 – Franchise Agreement

An FC agreement will be concluded between H/Q and the store owner. (FDD provided)Subsequent contracts for design/work/interior will be concluded.

Step 8 – Training

QSR Group carries out education for store owners, concerning management/use of information systems/confectionery/bakery/sanitation, etc..

Step 9 – Preparation for operation

Store owners proceed with permission and licensing procedures.
Store owners finish the construction and interior work.
QSR Group provides support for store owners in setting up and implementing a human resources plan.

Step 10 – Grand Opening

Finally, the store owner will open the store!
QSR Group will support the store owner with the opening and with promotional events.
